
# Automated Backups with Borgmatic

**Timeframe:** 2025  
**Tools:** Borg, Borgmatic, Cron, Discord Webhooks  

## Objective
Implement reliable, automated backups with monitoring and verification to protect critical system data.

## Implementation
- Scheduled backups using Borgmatic
- Encryption and compression enabled
- Retention policies to manage storage usage

## Monitoring & Alerts
- Discord webhook integration
- Notifications include:
  - Backup success/failure
  - Duration
  - Archive and repository size

## Validation
- Regular archive inspection
- Tested restore workflows to ensure recoverability

## Outcomes
- Hands-on experience with backup strategy design
- Understanding of retention policies and disaster recovery
- Confidence in data restoration procedures
